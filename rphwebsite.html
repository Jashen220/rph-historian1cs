History of Cordova

Barangay Poblacion is located in Cordova, a municipality in the province of Cebu, Philippines. Cordova is known for its rich cultural heritage, picturesque landscapes, and vibrant community life. Barangay Poblacion likely serves as the town center or central district of Cordova, hosting various local government offices, commercial establishments, and community facilities. It's an area where residents gather for various activities and where visitors can experience the local culture and way of life.

We conducted an interview with the Barangay Captain, Mr. Basillote, and he stated that the name Población itself means a center of a Municipality. It typically houses important institutions such as the barangay hall, health center, and possibly a public market. The area may also feature a plaza or park where residents gather for events, celebrations, or simply to socialize. However, the name Poblacion in the old times doesn’t exist as there were only three Barangays in Cordova until such time that many Sitios (parts of barangay) are introduced.
